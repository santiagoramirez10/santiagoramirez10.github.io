Guide to Age Of Empires II-Definitive Edition

Cheats

To do the cheats you must press the enter key and write the cheat you want to do.


  • cheese steak jimmy's: add 1000 food units.

  • robin hood: add 1000 gold units.

  • rock on: add 1000 rock units.

  • lumberjack: add 1000 wood units.

  • aegis: constructions and creations are immediate.

  • how do you turn this on: create a car that shoots stones.

  • wimpywimpywimpy: suicide.

  • natural wonders: it allows you to control the animals.

  • marco: show the full map.

  • polo: remove all the fog.

  • resign: lose the game immediately.

  • i r winner: instant victory.

  • black death: kill all opponents.

  • torpedo(1-8): kill the indicated opponent (you must indicate the number of the player).

  • to smithereens: create a saboteur.

  • i love the monkey head: you get the "Villager Male Dave Lewis" unit.

  • woof woof: create a flying dog with cape.

  • furious the monkey boy: create super building destroying monkeys.
